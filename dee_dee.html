HEY....I AM DEE DEE...

Dee Dee is one of the main villains (alongside Joey and Marky) in the series. He is named after the bassist of the punk rock group The Ramones, Dee Dee Ramone.Dee Dee is a cockroach who is always hungry and eats everything. He acts a bit childish and immature. He is also the most sensitive as he is seen crying over food, the loss of his brothers and even his failed attempts to appear in an opera show with Jack in one episode.
